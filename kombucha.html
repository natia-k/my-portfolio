FERMENT
COLOR IS THE BRAND

ONE FLAVOR. ONE COLOR.
NO DECORATION.

This project is a deliberate reverse of category expectations — no kraft, no hand-drawn botanicals, no “craft clichés.” Everything is stripped so the fermented color becomes the brand language itself.

The palette is drawn directly from fermentation — raw amber, hibiscus blush, blackcurrant near-black. Not colors about the flavors — colors from them.

PREMIUM WITHOUT PERFORMANCE.
INTENTIONAL WITHOUT ARTIFICE.

By removing all extraneous ornament and visual noise, the system creates a confident presence on shelf without trying too hard.

ROLE: CONCEPT, COLOR SYSTEM, LABEL DESIGN, TYPOGRAPHY, MOCKUPS
