NATIA STUDIO NURTURE

FEELINGS

a visual archive of feeling

I made a heart. I broke it. Fragments orbit me— tiny planets of pain that refuse to burn. The first time this sadness touches you it never leaves.
For this piece, I literally created a heart shape and broke it. The fragments became the orbiting pieces you see, like echoes of heartbreak.
Since I was five I have stood at the edge of sleep. Wanting to jump. Terrified of the splash. I speak to the dark. It watches. Sleep is one step away— always a lifetime away.
I've struggled with this since I was five. Lately, I've learned to take it as a gift—longer days to watch the world quietly unfold.
Warmth turns into gravity. You fall upward. Willingly. Again and Again. A 1001 times, Alive, Infinite.
It arrives without sound. It swallows everything. Even your echo disappears. A black hole rises inside. It feels empty, but it is heavy. It can hold everything. You carry space alone.
Love is what remains after explanation leaves.
Desire is what moves before permission.
