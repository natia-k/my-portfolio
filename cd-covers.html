NATIACLOSE
PROJECT 06 / 2022

FEELINGS

A visual study of the subjective human condition. Capturing emotions before they fade into the bottomless void.

HEARTBREAK

"Fragments orbit me—tiny planets of pain that refuse to burn."

Created by forming a heart shape and literally breaking it into echoing fragments.

INSOMNIA

"Since I was five, I have stood at the edge of sleep. Wanting to jump."

A personal struggle transformed into a gift—extra time to watch the world unfold.

PLEASURE

"Warmth turns into gravity. You fall upward. Willingly."

An infinite state of satisfaction, 1001 times alive.

EMPTINESS

"It feels empty, but it is heavy. It can hold everything."

A bottomless void that arrives without sound and swallows every echo.
