FERMENT

Self-initiated packaging concept | 2025
Color is the flavor. A packaging concept built on simplicity, presence, and clarity. I stripped everything back and let color become the identity. The palette is drawn from fermentation: raw amber, hibiscus blush, blackcurrant near-black — not colors about flavor, but colors from flavor.
