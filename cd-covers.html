Project Series 06

FEELINGS

Everything is constant. You are already feeling something. Sometimes there is a reason—a memory, a smell, a person—but the feeling is always there. These are the artifacts of those moments.

HEARTBREAK

"Fragments orbit me—tiny planets of pain that refuse to burn."

Digital Illustration. 2022. I created a heart and literally broke it. These fragments are the echoes that never leave once sadness touches you.

INSOMNIA

"Since I was five, I have stood at the edge of sleep. Wanting to jump. Terrified of the splash."

A lifelong struggle reframed as a gift. Extra hours to watch the world quietly unfold while the dark watches back.

PLEASURE

"Warmth turns into gravity. You fall upward. Willingly. Again and again."

An infinite, 1001-fold state of satisfaction. Alive and repetitive. A fall into the sun.

EMPTINESS

"It feels empty, but it is heavy. It can hold everything. You carry space alone."

The bottomless void. It arrives without sound and swallows every echo. A black hole rising inside.
