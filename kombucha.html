NATIA KURTANIDZE

FERMENT

Self-initiated packaging concept | 2025
Color is the flavor. A study in presence and restraint. This concept removes category noise — no kraft paper, no botanical clichés. Instead, the identity arises from the liquid itself. One flavor. One color. Uncompromising simplicity.

The palette is taken from fermentation: raw amber, hibiscus blush, and blackcurrant near-black. The system prioritizes clarity, hierarchy, and shelf legibility. It feels bold because it leaves nothing unnecessary behind.
