Identity — Concept 01

FERMENT.

One Flavor. One Color.
We stripped away category clichés—no illustrations, no patterns—leaving only what the drink truly is: a raw biological process. The color of the fermented liquid itself becomes the brand language.

Visual Logic

Structural
Purity.

The identity system is built with absolute restraint—color, form, and typographic clarity that reveal what the product actually is, not what it pretends to be.

Chromatics

Extracted
Hues.

Chromatics drawn directly from the liquid: Raw Amber, Hibiscus Blush, Blackcurrant Onyx. No decorative noise—the beverage's inherent character is the primary visual source.
