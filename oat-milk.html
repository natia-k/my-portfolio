NATIA KURTANIDZE

OAT

Self-initiated packaging concept | 2025
A deliberate study in structural restraint. We treated the carton as a monolith — removing category noise so form and material could speak. The visual system is built around clarity, simplicity, and scale, prioritizing information hierarchy without decoration.

The palette comes directly from the essence of the liquid itself — warm cream tones that feel natural yet distinct. Intentional structure creates a presence that feels calm and confident on shelf.
