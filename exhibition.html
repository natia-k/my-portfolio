Circle of Life

ILLUSTRATION / 2025
"Tiny pieces of ancient suns gathered for a moment into a body, a breath, a heartbeat. We don’t disappear; we fall back into the universe."
This piece is the whole circle in one glow: becoming, shining, dissolving, returning. Little stars walking on a planet made of older stars—finding our way back to the light that made us.

Nostalgia

DIGITAL PRINT / 2024
"That sharp ache of sadness. Being an outsider in your own past life; never fully here, never fully there."
An exploration of temporal displacement. A shadow of who you were, standing in a room that no longer exists. Memory is a ghost that refuses to leave the house.
