NATIA STUDIO CONTACT
CHROMATIC
TYPE Concept / Self-initiated
ROLE Concept, Color, Label, Packaging
The Brief

Opposite of the shelf.

A self-initiated concept for a small-batch kombucha brand. No kraft paper, no hand-drawn botanicals — the opposite of every other brand on the shelf.

The Decision

One flavor, one color.

I stripped everything back and let the product color become the personality. Full commitment to the fermentation palette.

Chromatic Soul

Raw Amber & Hibiscus Blush.

The palette came from fermentation itself. Not colors about the flavors, but colors from them. Designed for recognition and shelf legibility.

The Result

Intentional & Honest.

A visual identity that feels premium without trying to look expensive. Bold, expressive, and differentiated on the shelf.
