The Concept

Structural
Purity.

In a category crowded with rustic illustrations and "farm-to-table" clichés, this design chooses architectural silence. We treated the milk carton not as a grocery item, but as a structural monolith.

Visual Logic

Chromatics
of Cream.

The palette is restricted to Oat Cream and Deep Charcoal. By removing the "visual noise" of typical packaging, the focus shifts entirely to the quality of the material and the clarity of the type.
