FEELINGS

a visual archive of feeling

I made a heart. I broke it. Fragments orbit me— tiny planets of pain.
The fragments became the orbiting pieces you see, like echoes of heartbreak.
Since I was five I have stood at the edge of sleep. Wanting to jump.
Lately, I've learned to take it as a gift—longer days to watch the world.
